# Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how the Vouch Bot collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you interact with the bot on Telegram.

## 1. Information We Collect
The bot may collect and store:

1. Telegram usernames and user IDs involved in vouch activity
2. Vouch messages and reasons submitted through bot commands
3. Timestamps of submitted vouches
4. Blacklist records, including reason and moderator/admin attribution
5. Group or chat identifiers when actions are triggered in chats
6. Basic usage data required to process bot commands and reputation features

## 2. How Information Is Used
The collected information is used to:

1. Record and display vouches and reputation history
2. Enforce cooldowns, limits, and moderation actions
3. Show user profiles, rankings, recent vouches, and search results
4. Broadcast configured vouch actions to designated Telegram chats/channels
5. Maintain safety, abuse prevention, and basic bot functionality

## 3. Data Storage

1. Bot data may be stored locally or on the hosting environment using a database or similar storage system
2. Data may remain stored until deleted by the bot operator or removed through future administrative tools
3. No guarantee is made that deleted Telegram usernames or changed usernames will automatically update historical records

## 4. Data Sharing
Information may be shared in the following ways:

1. Vouches and related details may be shown to users through bot commands
2. Some actions may be broadcast to a configured Telegram group/channel
3. Moderation-related information may be visible to admins or bot operators
4. Data is not sold to third parties

## 5. User-Generated Content
Users are responsible for the content they submit through the bot, including vouch text, reasons, and usernames. Do not submit sensitive personal information through bot commands.

## 6. Legal and Safety Disclosure
Information may be reviewed, restricted, or disclosed if necessary to:

1. Enforce platform rules or group rules
2. Investigate abuse, fraud, impersonation, or harassment
3. Comply with applicable legal obligations

## 7. Security
Reasonable efforts may be made to protect stored data, but no system can guarantee absolute security. Users should avoid sharing confidential or highly sensitive information through the bot.

## 8. Children's Privacy
This bot is not intended for users who are prohibited from using Telegram under Telegram's own terms or applicable law.

## 9. Your Choices
Depending on how the bot is operated, users may be able to:

1. Avoid interacting with the bot
2. Request review or removal of certain bot records by contacting the bot operator
3. Leave chats where the bot is active

## 10. Changes to This Policy
This Privacy Policy may be updated at any time. Continued use of the bot after updates means the updated policy may apply.

## 11. Contact
For privacy-related questions, data concerns, or removal requests, contact the bot owner/operator: New Tele Network.
